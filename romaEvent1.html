colosseum

The Colosseum is an elliptical amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome, Italy, just east of the Roman Forum. It is the largest ancient amphitheatre ever built, and is still the largest standing amphitheatre in the world, despite its age.

schedule

step 1

We'll meet in front of the Colosseum's main entrance gate at noon. If you haven't already purchased tickets, we'll head to the nearby official ticket office to get them together. Remember, I can help recommend the best ticket options for our group's needs.

step 2

We'll start by stepping onto the Arena Floor, where the gladiatorial battles once took place. Imagine the roar of the crowd and the clashing of swords!

step 3

I'll allocate 30 minutes for you to roam freely and explore the Colosseum at your own pace. Take photos, revisit your favorite spots, or simply soak in the atmosphere.
