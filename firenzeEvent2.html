Uffizi Gallery

The Uffizi Gallery is a prominent art museum located adjacent to the Piazza della Signoria in the Historic Centre of Florence in the region of Tuscany, Italy.

schedule

step 1

The Uffizi has a vast collection of Renaissance masterpieces. Decide if you want a general overview or to focus on specific artists (like Botticelli, Michelangelo, or Leonardo da Vinci).

step 2

Arrive at the Uffizi Gallery slightly before your designated ticket time. Use the designated entrance based on your ticket type.

step 3

For in-depth historical context, consider pre-booking a guided tour or renting an audio guide. Take breaks to avoid art fatigue and enjoy views from the gallery windows overlooking the city.
