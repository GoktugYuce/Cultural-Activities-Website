St. Peter's Basilica

The Papal Basilica of Saint Peter in the Vatican, or simply Saint Peter's Basilica, is a church of the Italian High Renaissance located in Vatican City, an independent microstate enclaved within the city of Rome, Italy.

schedule

step 1

Make your way to the vast St. Peter's Square. You can use public transportation or walk if staying nearby. Join the security line, which often wraps around part of the square. Be prepared for waits, especially during peak season.

step 2

Once through security, enter the awe-inspiring basilica. Take a moment to appreciate the sheer scale and beauty.

step 3

Visit the grottoes (underground level) to see the tombs of numerous popes and other noteworthy figures. If interested, explore the Treasury museum (fee required), which houses liturgical objects and artifacts.
